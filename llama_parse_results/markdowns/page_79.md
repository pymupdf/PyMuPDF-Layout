## 3.3.1 Features

IBM Cloud Pak for Automation is a containerized automation software platform with pre-integrated automation capabilities such as workflow and decision automation, content management, document processing, and operational intelligence (Figure 3-7 on page 43). This enables organizations to digitize all styles of work with AI-infused business-oriented tools and built-in operational analytics for visibility and governance. This offering empowers business users to rapidly deliver applications and services at enterprise scale for greater cost savings and operational efficiencies. The IBM Cloud Pak for Automation can run anywhere (on-premises, on private and public clouds, and in pre-integrated systems) and is <ins>Red Hat OpenShift certified</ins>.
