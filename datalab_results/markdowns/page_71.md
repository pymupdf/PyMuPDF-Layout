In an environment with multiple IBM Storwize V7000 clusters, you can easily direct the onsite personnel or technician to the correct device by enabling the identification LED on the front pane. Click **Turn Identify On** in the menu that is shown in Figure 5-31.

![Figure 5-31 shows the Identification LED feature. The left pane displays a Front View of the drives. A right-click menu is shown over one drive, highlighting 'Turn Identify On'. An arrow points from this menu selection to the right pane, which shows the same Front View but with the identification LED on the selected drive flashing yellow, indicating it has been turned on.](5fb340ad68b0c71df0b56698b137e35b_img.jpg)

Figure 5-31 shows the Identification LED feature. The left pane displays a Front View of the drives. A right-click menu is shown over one drive, highlighting 'Turn Identify On'. An arrow points from this menu selection to the right pane, which shows the same Front View but with the identification LED on the selected drive flashing yellow, indicating it has been turned on.

Figure 5-31 Identification LED

Wait for confirmation from the technician that the device in the data center was correctly identified. In the GUI, you see a flashing light, which indicates that the Identify LED was turned on.
