The chart shows Henry Hub natural gas spot prices from December 2000 to December 2010, displaying both monthly price bars (blue) and a price trend line (orange). Natural gas prices experienced significant volatility, with notable spikes reaching around $15/MMBtu in late 2005 and over $15/MMBtu in 2008. The charts show ten-year stock performance comparisons from 2005-2010 (left) and 2000-2010 (right) for CHK, a 2010 peer group of energy companies, and the S&P 500. CHK and its peer group experienced significant volatility, with dramatic peaks around 2007-2008 followed by sharp declines, while the S&P 500 showed more moderate fluctuations.
