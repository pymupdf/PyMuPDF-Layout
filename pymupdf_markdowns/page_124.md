(57) **ABSTRACT**


The present invention relates to a cross-linker for use in a
photoresist which is suitable for a photolithography process
using KrF (248 ru), ArF (193 nm), E-beam, ion beam or
EUV light source. According to the present invention, preferred cross-linkers comprise a copolymer having repeating
units derived from: (i) a compound represented by following
Chemical Formula 1 and/or (ii) one or more compound(s)
selected from the group consisting of acrylic acid, methacrylic acid and maleic anhydride.
