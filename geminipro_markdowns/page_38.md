# AIRSPACE INFORMATION (Continued)

## Special Flight Rules Area (SFRA)

![Special Flight Rules Area Symbol](sfra-symbol.png)
**Figure Description:** A blue border composed of square "teeth" (crenellated line).
> **Boxed Note:** SPECIAL FEDERAL AVIATION REGULATIONS (SFAR) - 14 CFR Part 93, Subpart U and SFAR 50.2 - GRAND CANYON NATIONAL PARK SPECIAL FLIGHT RULES AREA, Special regulations apply to all aircraft operations below 18,000 feet MSL.

---

## Special Use Airspace

Only the airspace effective below 18,000 feet MSL is shown. The type of area shall be spelled out in large areas if space permits.

### PROHIBITED, RESTRICTED or WARNING AREA

![Prohibited, Restricted, or Warning Area Symbol](prohibited-area.png)
**Figure Description:** A blue border with inward-facing hash marks (comb-like).
**Example Text inside area:** P-56 OR R-6401 OR W-518

### ALERT AREA

![Alert Area Symbol](alert-area.png)
**Figure Description:** A magenta border with inward-facing hash marks.
**Example Text inside area:** ALERT AREA A-631 CONCENTRATED STUDENT HELICOPTER TRAINING

*   \* Alert Areas do not extend into Class A, B, C and D airspace, or Class E airport surface areas.

### MILITARY OPERATIONS AREA (MOA)

![MOA Symbol](moa-symbol.png)
**Figure Description:** A magenta border with inward-facing hash marks.
**Example Text inside area:** VANCE 2 MOA (VANCE 2 MOA EXCLUDES AIRSPACE 1500' AGL AND BELOW).
