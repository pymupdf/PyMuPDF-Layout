# AIRSPACE INFORMATION (Continued)

## Special Flight Rules Area (SFRA)

![Symbol: Special Flight Rules Area](symbol-sfra.png)

**Symbol Description:** A blue border line with square teeth pointing inward.

**Boxed Note:**
> **SPECIAL FEDERAL AVIATION REGULATIONS (SFAR)** - 14 CFR Part 93, Subpart U and SFAR 50.2 - GRAND CANYON NATIONAL PARK SPECIAL FLIGHT RULES AREA, Special regulations apply to all aircraft operations below 18,000 feet MSL.

---

## Special Use Airspace

Only the airspace effective below 18,000 feet MSL is shown.  
The type of area shall be spelled out in large areas if space permits.

![Symbol: Prohibited, Restricted, or Warning Area](symbol-prohibited-restricted.png)

**Symbol Description:** Blue border with inward-pointing comb-like hash marks.  
**Example Label:** P-56 OR R-6401 OR W-518  
**Caption:** PROHIBITED, RESTRICTED or WARNING AREA

*Alert Areas do not extend into Class A, B, C and D airspace, or Class E airport surface areas.*

![Symbol: Alert Area](symbol-alert-area.png)

**Symbol Description:** Magenta border with inward-pointing comb-like hash marks.  
**Example Label:** ALERT AREA A-631 CONCENTRATED STUDENT HELICOPTER TRAINING  
**Caption:** ALERT AREA

![Symbol: MOA](symbol-moa.png)

**Symbol Description:** Magenta border with inward-pointing comb-like hash marks.  
**Example Label:** VANCE 2 MOA (VANCE 2 MOA EXCLUDES AIRSPACE 1500' AGL AND BELOW)  
**Caption:** MILITARY OPERATIONS AREA (MOA)

## Special Air Traffic Rules / Airport Patterns (FAR Part 93)

Appropriate boxed note as required shown adjacent to area.

Inside the FAR Part 93 boundary area, the cross hatching is at a 45 degree angle. The hypsometric tint shall be masked within the area around the yellow city tint when applicable (should not be confused with white glacier tint).

![Symbol: Special Air Traffic Rules](symbol-far-part-93.png)

**Symbol Description:** A rectangular area filled with diagonal blue hatching (45-degree angle).

**Boxed Note:**
> **SPECIAL NOTICE**  
> Pilots are required to obtain an ATC clearance prior to entering this area.

## National Security Area

Appropriate notes as required may be shown.

![Symbol: National Security Area](symbol-nsa.png)

**Small Area:** Represented by an irregular magenta shape.

**Boxed Note:**

## Special Awareness Training Areas

![Symbol: Special Awareness Training Area](symbol-training-area.png)

**Symbol Description:** A solid blue line interrupted by blue circles.  
**Example Label:** DCA VOR-DME 60 NM

**Boxed Note:**
> **NOTICE**  
> Special awareness training required within 60 NM of DCA VOR-DME. See description on Flyway.
