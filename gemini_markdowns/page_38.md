## AIRSPACE INFORMATION (Continued)

### Special Flight Rules Area (SFRA)

![Special Flight Rules Area (SFRA) Symbol](sfra-symbol.png)

**SFRA Note:** SPECIAL FEDERAL AVIATION REGULATIONS (SFAR) 14 CFR Part 93, Subpart U and SFAR 50,2 - GRAND CANYON NATIONAL PARK SPECIAL FLIGHT RULES AREA. Special regulations apply to all aircraft operations below 18,000 feet MSL.

### Special Use Airspace

Only the airspace effective below 18,000 feet MSL is shown.

The type of area shall be spelled out in large areas if space permits.

*   Alert Areas do not extend into Class A, B, C and D airspace, or Class E airport surface areas.

#### Prohibited, Restricted or Warning Area

![Prohibited, Restricted or Warning Area Symbol](prohibited-restricted-warning-area-symbol.png)

This symbol represents a Prohibited, Restricted or Warning Area, identified by designations such as P-56, R-6401, or W-518.

#### Alert Area

![Alert Area A-631 Symbol](alert-area-a-631-symbol.png)

This symbol depicts an Alert Area, for example, A-631, which indicates Concentrated Student Helicopter Training.

![Alert Area Vance 2 MOA Symbol](alert-area-vance-2-moa-symbol.png)

This symbol shows an Alert Area designated as Vance 2 MOA, specifically noting that it excludes airspace 1500' AGL and below. This also falls under Military Operations Area (MOA) designation.

### Special Awareness Training Areas

![Special Awareness Training Area Symbol DEA-VOR-DME-60-NM](special-awareness-training-area-symbol.png)

This symbol, e.g., "DEA-VOR-DME-60-NM-", denotes a Special Awareness Training Area.

**Training Area Notice:** Special awareness training required within 60 NM of DCA VOR-DME. See description on Flyway.

### Special Air Traffic Rules / Airport Patterns (FAR Part 93)

Appropriate boxed note as required shown adjacent to area.

Inside the FAR Part 93 boundary area, the cross hatching is at a 45 degree angle. The hypsometric tint shall be masked within the area around the yellow city tint when applicable (should not be confused with white glacier tint).

![Special Notice for Airport Patterns Box](airport-patterns-special-notice-box.png)

**Special Notice:** Pilots are required to obtain an ATC clearance prior to entering this area.
